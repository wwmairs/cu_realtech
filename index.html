Our Mission “Our mission is to prepare students to add immediate and long-term value to the real estate sector by teaching “where the industry is headed”, with a particular emphasis on identifying the roles of, and opportunities from, advances in all forms of technology.” The CU Real Estate Center of the Leeds School of Business aspires to be the nation’s leading center of education, knowledge and engagement for the real estate profession.Real estate, as a factor of production and a major financial investment asset, is one of the sectors most impacted by technology. In spite of this, real estate has been one of the slowest business sectors and areas of professional study to get their arms and heads around these changes. The powerful connections between the academic and business communities, which define the CU Real Estate Center, position the Leeds School of Business to quickly and dynamically embrace the opportunities from studying the interrelated relationships between technological change and real estate while simultaneously tackling the challenges arising from this accelerating evolution.
